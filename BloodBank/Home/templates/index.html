Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is essential to save lives in hospitals and emergency situations.

The Donation Process

Each donation can save multiple lives. If you're donating whole blood, we'll cleanse an area on your arm and insert a brand new sterile needle for the blood draw. (This feels like a quick pinch and is over in seconds.) Other types of donations, such as platelets, are made using an apheresis machine which will be connected to both arms.

Understanding Blood Types

Discover how your blood type affects who you can donate to and receive from.

World Blood Donors Day

Every year, on 14 June, countries around the world celebrate World Blood Donor Day to raise awareness of the need for safe blood and blood products and to thank blood donors for their life-saving gifts of blood.
